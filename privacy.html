Privacy Notice

Last updated: January 2026

Developed by: Ti Wu and Hyuntaek Oh

This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Notice.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name or nickname
  • Email address
  • Player profile details (e.g., height, dominant hand, position)
  • Feedback or support messages

You may choose not to provide certain information, but some features may be unavailable.

B. Video & Motion Data

If you choose to upload or record videos for analysis:

  • Basketball shooting videos
  • Extracted body keypoints and motion data derived from the video

Important:

• We do not perform facial recognition

• We do not identify individuals

• Videos are used only for performance analysis

C. Automatically Collected Data

We may automatically collect limited technical data such as:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage events (e.g., feature usage, crashes)
  • Anonymous identifiers for analytics

This data is used only to improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Analyze basketball shooting mechanics
  • Provide AI-generated performance feedback
  • Improve model accuracy and app experience
  • Maintain app functionality and security
  • Respond to support requests

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

3. AI & Machine Learning Usage

Our App uses AI and computer-vision models to analyze basketball shooting form.

  • Models process uploaded videos or derived motion data
  • AI outputs are performance feedback only
  • Data is not used for identity recognition
  • Training datasets are anonymized when used

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Videos and analysis results are stored only as long as necessary to provide the service
  • Users may delete their videos and data at any time
  • Deleted data is permanently removed from active systems within a reasonable timeframe

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data.

We may share limited data only with:

  • Secure cloud service providers (e.g., storage, analytics)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

All third-party services are required to follow strict confidentiality and security obligations.

6. Children's Privacy

  • The App is not directed at children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
  • If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent

Requests can be made by contacting us directly.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access controls

No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve protections.

9. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time.
  • Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Notice:

Email: support@monona-sports.com
